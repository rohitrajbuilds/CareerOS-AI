# CareerOS AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-07

CareerOS AI collects and processes profile data, resume data, job application context, and operational telemetry to provide autofill, AI-assisted drafting, resume analysis, company research, and application tracking features.

## Data we process

- Account information such as name and email
- Profile data entered by the user
- Resume uploads, parsed text, and derived analysis
- Application tracking records and job metadata
- AI request and response payloads needed to fulfill product actions
- Security, audit, and performance logs

## How we use data

- Provide product functionality requested by the user
- Improve reliability, safety, and support
- Prevent abuse and satisfy legal obligations

## Sharing

- Infrastructure vendors acting as processors
- AI providers used to fulfill user-initiated generation or analysis
- Legal or security disclosures when required

## User rights

- Update or delete profile data
- Remove local extension data by uninstalling the extension
- Request deletion through support@careeros.ai
