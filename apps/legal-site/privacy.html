Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-07. This policy covers the CareerOS AI Chrome extension, supporting APIs, and the hosted legal site.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, and authentication metadata.
  • Profile data you provide, including phone, links, education, work history, and sponsorship status.
  • Resume files, resume text, resume tags, and derived resume analysis artifacts.
  • Job application data such as company, role, source link, form field context, application status, and notes.
  • AI interaction data needed to generate requested drafts, analysis, and structured form answers.
  • Operational telemetry such as error logs, performance data, and security audit events.

How we use information

  • Provide autofill, answer generation, resume analysis, company research, and application tracking features.
  • Store your profile and application history so the product remains useful across sessions and devices.
  • Detect abuse, secure the platform, troubleshoot failures, and improve reliability.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce platform terms.

How data is shared

  • With infrastructure and hosting providers that process data on our behalf, such as cloud hosting, database, logging, and caching services.
  • With AI model providers only to the extent necessary to fulfill a user-requested generation or analysis workflow.
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect the rights, security, and integrity of CareerOS AI and its users.

We do not sell personal information or use job-search browsing activity for unrelated advertising.

Storage and retention

Local extension data is stored in Chrome extension storage and sensitive profile payloads are encrypted before storage by the extension. Server-side data is retained as long as needed to provide the service, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your choices

  • You may update or delete profile data inside the product.
  • You may uninstall the extension at any time.
  • You may request account deletion or privacy support at support@careeros.ai.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@careeros.ai.
