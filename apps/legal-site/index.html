Privacy Policy Permissions Explanation

What CareerOS AI does

CareerOS AI helps users manage job applications across supported hiring systems, generate draft answers, analyze resumes, and track application progress.

The product is designed around user-initiated workflows and does not collect or sell browsing history for unrelated advertising purposes.
